Special events at the Museum

We often have special events in a limited period of time. Please check this page for updated schedule.

  • Aeronautics lectures

    It is our pleasure to announce that Prof Sheila Widnall from the Massachusetts Institute of Technology will be delivering 3 lectures on the development of aeronautics and where the future lies in this exciting ‘space’.

    The lectures will take place on december 20, 21 and 22.

  • Night in the museum

    Get your family together for an exciting night in the museum as you sleep over beside dinosaurs and science displays. Bring your own sleeping bag and get ready to rough it as we go exploring the wonders of science.

    We arrange this sleepovers every wednesday.

  • Energetica exhibition

    On loan from the NEMO Science Museum in Amsterdam, the Energetica exhibition is coming to the Community Science Museum!

    It’s a series of installations that allow visitors to experience the power of the elements as we harness them. From solar energy powering lighting, to ‘Wind Island’ that shows how turbines are able to use and control wind to create power.

    The exhibition is available all December.

Contact us for more information

We are a community museum focusing on bringing science to all ages. Our exhibitions are made with exploration in mind, to enable learning by doing. All our exhibitions are free to explore. Guided tours once an hour for a small fee.
